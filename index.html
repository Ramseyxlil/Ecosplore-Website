Our Eco-System.

The word Eco came from the Latin word “oikos”,

which means household. An Ecosystem is the geographical Area

where plants, animals and other creatures work together

to form a bubble of life


Our Ecosystem contains the Land,marine and Freshwater

such as ponds, Lakes, underwater, Wetlands, Pools,

oceans, streams, and Rivers.
